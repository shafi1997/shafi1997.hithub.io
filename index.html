Message From The Chairman

Muslim Aid Institute of Technology (MAIT) Jessore is a permanent project of Muslim Aid-UK under Education and Child Protection Program. It is one of technical institute among similar six institutes across the country of Muslim Aid. MAIT-Jessore has been working in Jessore district since 2007. We are affiliated institute from Bangladesh Technical Education Board (BTEB) and institute code is 33106. We are providing 4 year Diploma in Civil, Electrical and Computer technology. In future we will increase our technology. Besides this we are also providing skill development training of 3/6 month duration. At present 1150 students are studying at MAIT-Jessore. More than 4500 students have already completed skill development training course on different trades like computer, hardware and networking, outsourcing, AutoCad, engine mechanics, general electrician, mobile servicing, tailoring etc from MAIT-Jessore and they are in well position. Most of them are earning sufficient and supporting their families. A skill development training course has changed their lives. We have a child sponsorship Program called Rainbow Family. By this Program we support specifically education, food, healthcare, and clothing. We are also providing stipend to students and it is also our special Program. Occasionally we distribute free tools and give loan among graduates without any interest. We have partnership with different International, National and Local NGOs. MAIT-Jessore always maintains good communication with government institutes in national and local level. We have 30 talented instructors and 12 energetic office staffs. Education department and Muslim Aid Bangladesh Field Office are always guiding us to ensure quality education. We all are concern for career development of students. We believe it is our holy duty to help students for better carrier.
Chairman
MAIT - Jessore
